About

PenguinSite is my first attempt at something I would consider a real website. It is meant to have a light hearted feel, but a clean design where pages follow a consistent order of header/content/footer. I have always thought of penguins as very peaceful animals, with a lighthearted nature, and loved by many. It was a joy finding the pictures and images used in this site, as well as finding out some lesser known things about the penguins as a whole. I hope you enjoyed viewing this site as much as I enjoyed putting it together.
-Ryan Swanson

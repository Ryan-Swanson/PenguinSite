Penguin Habitat

Video by Chris J Mitchell License by Pexels

The penguins shown in the video above are likely where one would come across a penguin, at the zoo. While common depiction of a penguin is the arctic, this isn't where the majority live. They like the cold, however they will not be found in the arctic. They can be found as far north as the Galapagos Islands, where they survive due to the cold waters of the Antarctic Humboldt Current.
